
You can retrieve an up-to-date copy of the Linpack Benchmark report in 
postscript form by:

1) use a web browser with the url http://www.netlib.org/liblist.html, 
   click on "benchmark", click on "benchmark/performance.ps".

2) anonymous ftp to netlib2.cs.utk.edu
     cd benchmark
     get performance.ps
     quit

3) sending email to netlib@netlib.org and in the message type:
     send performance.ps from benchmark

Hope this helps,
